The Photographer

Candid stories, told with quiet elegance

Chandan Venigella is a wedding, destination and editorial photographer based in Hyderabad, with over thirteen years spent documenting love stories across India and beyond.

Every frame is built around real emotion over posed perfection — the glance before the vows, the hush before the first dance, the light exactly as it fell. Over 700 weddings later, that instinct for the unscripted moment is still what the work is built on.
